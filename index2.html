Virtualizing 3D objects is awesome! We develop new methods of optical 3D measurements based on the structured light technique and apply them in cultural heritage conservation, medicine and arts.

We're a research team based at the Warsaw University of Technology, being MSc and PhD students, and post-docs. We build our knowledge base since early 90s and now we're moving fast!
